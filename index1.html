The Watch Tour

What is a watch tour?

The concept of watch tour came about as an idea on the popular subreddit "r/Watches" and several Facebook watch groups such as Properly Wound and Independent Watch Aficionados. To begin a tour members of the comminity post watches they're interested in. A poll is then created to determine the most popular option. Once a watch is selected the entire group is asked to sumbit payment based on the number of participants. Tours consist of 10 people however there is no real limit.
To begin the process the winner who submitted the poll option will be able to confirm their place on the tour list e.g., first, last, etc. Once confirmed the watch begins the tour. It is sent from user to user based on an agreed upon time frame, usually 2-3 weeks per person. Each person is encouraged to take a photos of the watch. Users are also encouraged to post reviews and their impressions of the watch. Shipping logistics are determined based on user proximity as the tour progresses. At the end of the tour all participants are entered into a raffle to win the watch. The winner of the raffle can accept the offer, or put the watch up for sale at-or-below the initial purchase price.
