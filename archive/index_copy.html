The Watch Tour

What is a watch tour?

The concept of watch tour came about as an idea on the popular subreddit "r/Watches" and several Facebook watch groups such as Properly Wound and Independent Watch Aficionados. To begin a tour, members of the comminity would post watches they are interested in and would then open a poll to determine a watch they all agree on. Once a watch is selected, the entire group is ask to commit to equally fund of the price of the watch. Ideally a tour consists of 10 watches, however there is no real limit.To begin the process, the winner of the poll will be able to confirm their place on the tour list -- first, last, etc. This watch would then be sent from user to user after the agreed upon time frame, usually 2-3 weeks. Each user is encouraged to take a photo (wrist check) and photos of the watch out "in the wild." Users are also encouraged to post reviews and impressions of the watch. Shipping logistics would be determine based on user proximity as the tour progresses. At the end of the tour, all participants of the tour are entered into a raffle to win the watch. The winner of the raffle can accept the offer, or put the watch up for sale at-or-below market rates.
